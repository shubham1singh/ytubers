About Project

YTUBERS is a platform that connects businesses and companies with YouTubers for advertising and collaboration purposes. With Ytubers, businesses and companies can easily find and hire YouTubers that match their target audience, and vice versa. This platform allows for a more streamlined process for both parties, eliminating the need for time-consuming searches and negotiations.

Main Goal

The main goal of Ytubers is to provide a convenient and efficient platform for businesses and companies to reach their target audience through YouTubers, while also allowing YouTubers to monetize their channel by offering advertising and collaboration opportunities. This platform can benefit both parties by providing a new source of revenue for YouTubers and a costeffective means of advertising for businesses and companies.
